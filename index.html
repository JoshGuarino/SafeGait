SafeGait is designed, developed and manufactured by Gorbel Medical (a division of Gorbel Inc.). SafeGait’s Integrated Rail System and G-Force® technologies are based on almost 40 years of Gorbel’s patented technologies and innovations in designing, building and installing cranes, custom rails and safety solutions.
